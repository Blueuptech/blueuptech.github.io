# CISO experience

Throughout my career, I have successfully led the response to critical infrastructure breaches caused by advanced persistent threats. In one notable instance, an external company collaborated with an entity within the banking group where I served as Global CISO, compromising the access permissions of one of its users. This access was acquired by the Play ransomware group, who launched an attack after exfiltrating information from the entity, intending to use it for extortion.

# CISO experience

Due to the conflict in Ukraine, I, as CISO, had to manage numerous attacks, particularly DoS, on a periodic basis and monitor communication channels used by these groups where they often published their intentions.

I led the formulation of a comprehensive defense strategy that leveraged threat intelligence and enhanced network segmentation. By employing deception tactics and threat hunting, we successfully neutralized the attackers and bolstered our cyber resilience.

In one notable instance, an external company collaborated with an entity within the banking group where I served as Global CISO, compromising the access permissions of one of its users. This access was acquired by the Play ransomware group, who launched an attack after exfiltrating information from the entity, intending to use it for extortion.

## Successful Response to a Major Security Incident

The detection occurred after the attack's launch, as the alerts associated with indicators of compromise in this area were not correctly configured, primarily due to the lack of communication regarding their existence by the compromised entity. Containment was swift and localized, allowing us to eliminate the compromised production environment while creating an isolated one for forensic analysis and an additional one for deploying new services once confirmed uncompromised. Notably, we did not utilize Active Directory backups but recreated them from scratch.

An immediate crisis management organization was established, enabling us to inform internal and external stakeholders, including regulators and customers, about the incident. This transparent approach allowed for regular updates without engaging a specialized communication agency.

The keys to our success in this incident response were swift action, thorough forensic analysis, and effective team communication. By isolating affected systems, eradicating the threat, and restoring operations with minimal downtime, we effectively mitigated the impact of the attack. Future improvements could include enhanced threat detection through improved monitoring and asset discovery, as well as AI-driven anomaly detection

## Coordination with Public Agency

I have a proven track record of successfully coordinating our company's security operations with national cybersecurity agencies such as INCIBE, CNPIC, and CCN during widespread malware campaigns and orchestrated DoS attacks. This collaboration involved sharing threat intelligence, adopting recommended protective measures, and participating in joint briefings. These experiences have emphasized the importance of public-private partnerships in enhancing collective defense mechanisms against cyber threats.

During my tenure as Global CISO, I also engaged with various regulatory and governmental organizations to ensure compliance and enhance security. These included the Bank of Spain (Banco de España) for regulatory compliance and financial security, the Data Protection Agency (Agencia de Protección de Datos) for adherence to data privacy laws, and the National Center for the Protection of Critical Infrastructures (Centro Nacional de Protección de Infraestructuras Críticas - CNPIC) for safeguarding essential services and infrastructure.

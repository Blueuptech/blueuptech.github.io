## Development Methodologies Experience

I have extensive experience with Agile, DevOps, and Waterfall methodologies, with a preference for Agile and DevOps due to their flexibility, speed, and emphasis on collaboration. These methodologies allow quick adaptation to changes, deliver incremental value, and promote continuous improvement, streamlining our development processes.

- Agile:
	- Scrum
	- Kanban
	- Lean
- Waterfall:
	- Traditional Waterfall
	- V-Model
	- Incremental Waterfall
- DevOps:
	- Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD)
	- Infrastructure as Code (IaC)
	- Continuous Monitoring and Feedback   
- Rapid Application Development (RAD):
	- Prototyping
	- Iterative Development
	- User-Centered Design
- Extreme Programming (XP):
	- Test-Driven Development (TDD)
	- Pair Programming
	- Continuous Integration    
- Lean Startup:
	- Build-Measure-Learn Feedback Loop
	- Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
	- Customer Development
